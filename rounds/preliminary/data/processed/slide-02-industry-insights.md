# Slide 2 Processed Insights: Industry Change And Relationship Reconstruction

## Slide Thesis

> 美妆增长逻辑正在从“卖产品”转向“建关系”：Z 世代不再只是购买产品，而是在购买“我如何被看见”的表达方式。

## Three Core Shifts

| From | To | Strategic implication |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 品牌定义美 | 用户定义自我 | 品牌需要参与用户表达，而不是单向灌输审美 |
| 单向种草转化 | 搜索、比较、评价、共创 | 品牌需要持续互动，而不是只追求一次转化 |
| 产品/符号购买 | 场景、情绪、身份购买 | 品牌需要理解用户当下状态，而不是只做人群标签 |

## Evidence To Use

1. Luxury market recovery is cautious; brands need personalization, culture, and authentic connection.
2. Bain/Vogue Business report that luxury experiences outperform personal goods in consumer sentiment and that Gen Z has low loyalty but high cultural influence.
3. Beauty shoppers show price sensitivity; Vogue Business Beauty Index notes that 31% of shoppers would move to less expensive brands if prices rise.
4. Beauty brands operate in a saturated digital attention field; pop-ups and UGC are important engagement tools.
5. Existing beauty AI cases often emphasize diagnostics, recommendations, filters, shade finders, makeup transfer, or service efficiency.
